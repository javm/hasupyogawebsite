Q&A

I’m a beginner, is this ok?
Absolutley! We welcome all levels and everyone. Your class will be tailored to the needs of the group.
What should I bring?
  • Swimming suit
  • Rash guard and/or other sun proctective clothing (there is no sunscreen of any kind allowed)
  • Water bottle
  • Towel
I’m not a great swimmer… is this a problem?
Definitely not. The waters we play on are shallow and if your booking is at a cenote, there are lifejackets to borrow if you want to venture into deeper waters.
What is a cenote?
A cenote is a fresh water ‘pool’ and they are found throughout the region. Some are open water pools, similar to a pond or small lake. Others are open but then go underground and are caves. Cenotes were sacred for ancient mayan people.
