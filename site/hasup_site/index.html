Stand Up Paddling Yoga

Have the yoga experience of your life on a beautiful carribean lagoon or cenote on a stand-up paddle board. We will arrange for hotel pick up and take you on an amazing yoga adventure. A beautiful and rejuvinating floating yoga class in a natural beauty, delicious snacks and beverages, and great company are just some of the things you will enjoy on this world class yoga adventure.


Join us for an amazing yoga experience surrounded by the natural beauty found in Tulum’s lagoons and cenotes

 

Create amazing memories with your very own SUP Yoga experience.


And make this one of the highlights of your time in Tulum.

You’ll be picked up from your hotel or private rental and escorted to a natural lagoon or cenote where you’ll be led through some basic paddleboard instructions before beginning your yoga class.

Your certified SUP teacher will guide you through an all-levels practice which will end with the most relaxing and rejuvenating savasana you’ve ever experienced. Imagine blue skies above with the healing energy of natural fresh water below.

One you finish your yoga experience you’ll have time to paddle and explore or head to the dock/dry land to soak up the sun. You’ll enjoy a healthy snack and natural ‘agua fresca’ before being escorted back to Tulum to enjoy the rest of your day.

Q&A

What should I expect?
You’ll enjoy a 60 minute guided yoga class with a certified yoga teacher and time to explore the cenote or lagoon. We encourage our yogis to leave their expectations at the door and practice being truly present in the experience.
I’m a beginner, is this ok?
We teach to all levels and every body. And you’re class will be tailored to accommodate the group and booking.
What should I wear?
Please wear a comfortable bathing suit that will support your movement on and off the board. If you tend to be sun sensitive, a rash guard or long sleeve and leggings are always great options. *If you choose to wear a hot or sunglasses, please make sure they are secured.
What else should I bring?
  • bottled water
  • eco-friendly sunscreen
  • towel & change of clothes (if needed)
  • PLEASE LEAVE VALUABLES AT YOOR HOTEL OR PRIVATE STAY
I’m not a great swimmer… is this a problem?
Definitely not. The waters we play on are shallow and if your booking is at a cenote, there are lifejackets to borrow if you want to venture into deeper waters.
What is a cenote?
A cenote is a fresh water ‘pool’ and they are found throughout the region. Some are open water pools, similar to a pond or small lake. Others are open but then go underground and are caves. Mayans believe the waters to have healing powers and due to the mineral content are super soothing to the skin.
